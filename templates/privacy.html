Privacy Policy for Lueur

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Lueur ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mental wellness application featuring Luna, an AI companion that provides supportive responses to your mood journal entries. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Lueur, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • A unique user identifier (managed by Firebase Authentication)
  • Optional profile information you choose to provide (full name, phone number, bio, date of birth, gender)

Account creation and sign-in are handled by Firebase Authentication, a service provided by Google. We do not directly store your password — Firebase manages this on our behalf. See Google's Firebase privacy documentation for details: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Mood Journal Content

When you use Lueur's journaling feature, we collect:

  • The mood emoji you select
  • The text you write about your thoughts and feelings ("journal entries")
  • Timestamps of when entries are created

AI-Generated Responses

Each journal entry you submit is processed to generate an empathetic response from Luna. To generate this response, your journal entry text is sent to Groq, a third-party AI processing service, for analysis. Groq processes this text solely to generate a response and return it to Lueur. We do not control Groq's independent data retention practices; you can review Groq's privacy policy at groq.com/privacy-policy.

We store both your original journal entry and Luna's AI-generated response in our database, associated with your account, so you can view your journal history.

Usage Data

We may automatically collect limited technical information, such as API request timestamps and error logs (used for debugging, via our error-monitoring service, Sentry). We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or advertising identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
  • Generate personalized, empathetic AI responses to your journal entries
  • Display your journal history back to you
  • Generate weekly summary letters based on your recent entries
  • Identify and fix technical issues (via error monitoring)

We do not: sell your data to third parties, use your journal content for advertising purposes, or display third-party advertisements in the app.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and journal entries for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account (see below), we permanently delete your journal entries and account data from our systems.

Your Rights and Choices

Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete Account). This action deletes your Firebase authentication identity, permanently deletes your account and all journal entries from our database, and cannot be undone.

Data Access and Correction

You may view and update your profile information (name, bio, phone number, date of birth, gender) at any time within the app.

Data Portability

If you would like a copy of your data before deletion, please contact us at riyam.thekluge@gmail.com.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmitted between the app and our servers, authentication via Firebase's secure token-based system, and access controls limiting who can access production data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Lueur is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at riyam.thekluge@gmail.com.

A note on sensitive content: Lueur is designed as a supportive wellness tool, not a substitute for professional support. Journal entries may contain sensitive personal information about your emotional state. We encourage you to be mindful of what you share and to seek help from a qualified professional if you are going through a crisis. Lueur is not equipped to respond to emergencies. If you are in crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.
